Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to rent a venue for a wedding at Domaine de Barneville?

The rental price for a wedding venue at Domaine de Barneville starts at €4,500 and can be customized (e.g., also available for installment payments). Feel free to get in touch, and we will be happy to discuss the details.

What facilities does Domaine de Barneville have for weddings?

Domaine de Barneville offers the following facilities and spaces for weddings:

  • Terrace
  • Garden
  • Swimming pool
  • Dance floor
  • Parking
  • Special area for the caterer located next to the Orangerie
  • Marquee
  • Area for a civil ceremony
  • Other (Tennis court)
What services does Domaine de Barneville offer for weddings?

Domaine de Barneville offers the following services for weddings:

  • Banquet
  • Ceremony
  • Other (We offer the rental of the estate for your events. You are free to choose your own service providers, but we can recommend trusted vendors we work with regularly.)
What seating configurations does Domaine de Barneville offer for the wedding reception?

Domaine de Barneville offers the following configurations:

  • Standing
  • Sitting
What types of receptions does Domaine de Barneville offer?

The different types of receptions offered by Domaine de Barneville include:

  • Reception
  • Welcome cocktail
  • Buffet
  • Dinner
  • Aperitif
How many people can benefit from the wedding reception services at Domaine de Barneville?

Domaine de Barneville offers its wedding reception services for a maximum of 170 guests.

How many users recommend Domaine de Barneville and what aspects of the wedding services are most highly rated?

Domaine de Barneville is recommended by 100% of the couples who used this provider's services. The overall rating is 5.0, awarded for the quality of the service, flexibility, value for money, professionalism, and response time.

Are there any special offers or discounts for wedding services?

Currently, there are no discount promotions, but Domaine de Barneville offers special prices for high and low seasons.

Is there a list of available wedding dates?

Currently, we do not provide a fixed list of available dates. Please fill out the form, and we will get back to you as soon as possible to check the availability for your desired date.

How long can we rent Domaine de Barneville for our wedding?

We usually rent Domaine de Barneville for an entire weekend. However, if you wish to rent it for a different time frame, we can discuss and arrange it individually.

When can we start setting up and decorating?

The time for setup and decoration can be arranged individually after discussing with us. We are flexible and will find a solution that works for you.

Do we have to take care of the teardown, or is it included in the service?

Teardown must be done by you, but we have good contacts with reliable service providers who we can recommend to make the teardown process as easy as possible for you.

Are there enough parking spaces for all our guests?

Yes, we have a very large parking area with plenty of space for all your guests.

Does Domaine de Barneville offer a shuttle service for guests who can't come by car?

Unfortunately, we do not offer a shuttle service, but we can refer you to suitable providers who can assist with transportation for your guests.

Is Domaine de Barneville easily accessible by public transport for guests coming from afar?

Domaine de Barneville is easily accessible: Deauville Airport is just 10 minutes away, and Deauville-Trouville's main train station offers direct connections from Paris. Alternatively, guests can fly into Paris' CDG or Orly airports, both around 2.5 hours away.

What is included in the rental price? Is catering and drinks included or do they need to be booked separately?

Catering and drinks are not included in the rental price and must be arranged separately. However, we are happy to help you find a great caterer through our contacts and recommendations.

Are there different wedding packages and what is included in each?

We do not currently offer fixed wedding packages. We work with you to create the perfect package tailored to your needs. Let us know what you're looking for, and we will help you make it happen!

Do you offer additional services such as a wedding planner or a decoration service?

We do not offer wedding planners or decoration services ourselves, but we can refer you to reliable partners with whom we've had great experiences.

What happens if it rains on our wedding day? Is there a backup plan for the ceremony or reception?

In case of bad weather, we provide tents that are waterproof, so your ceremony and reception can still go ahead without worry. A little tip: This is part of the charm of Normandy – the rain is part of the experience!

Can we use the tent during the low season, or is it only available in the high season?

The tent is available all year round, including the low season. If you're planning a winter wedding or event, we can arrange it for you.

Can we bring our own decorations, or are there restrictions on decorating?

Of course, you are welcome to bring your own decorations! We want your wedding to be as personalized as possible and are happy if you set your own personal touches.

Do you have recommendations for florists, photographers, or DJs?

Yes, we have a good overview of vendors in the region with whom we have worked regularly. If you like, we can provide you with recommendations for florists, photographers, DJs, and other vendors who will make your wedding unforgettable.

Are there restrictions on lighting or music, such as volume or light design?

There are no restrictions on lighting or music – you can create the atmosphere however you like. There are no volume limits, and you can customize the lighting and music as you wish.

Are there hotels or accommodations nearby for our guests?

Yes, since Domaine de Barneville is located in a popular tourist area, there are many hotels and accommodations nearby. We can also provide you with a list of recommendations if you'd like.

Does Domaine de Barneville offer overnight accommodations or lodging?

Yes, we offer overnight accommodations for your guests. We'd be happy to share available options and help you find the best accommodations for your needs.

Can we bring our own wedding cake?

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own wedding cake. We want your wedding to be perfect and will support you in any way we can!

Are there any volume restrictions for music or the event?

No, there are no volume restrictions for your event. You can celebrate as loud as you like – Domaine de Barneville offers plenty of space for your carefree celebration!

How late can we celebrate? Is there a curfew?

There is no curfew! You can celebrate as long as you like – we want your wedding to be unforgettable in every way.

How does Domaine de Barneville handle safety precautions?

Domaine de Barneville ensures that all necessary safety precautions are in place so that your event runs safely and smoothly. There is always a contact person on site to assist with any safety-related concerns.

Can we hold a religious or cultural ceremony at Domaine de Barneville?

Yes, we offer the possibility to hold a religious or cultural ceremony as desired. Whether it's a civil ceremony or a traditional one – we are here to help make your dream wedding come true.

What technical equipment is available (speakers, microphones, projectors, etc.)?

Domaine de Barneville provides electricity and water hookups, but speakers, microphones, and projectors must be rented from external providers. We can help connect you with the right contacts for this equipment.

Who is the on-site contact person?

We ensure that there is always an on-site contact person who will assist you during the planning and throughout the event. You won't have to worry about anything – we're here for you!
